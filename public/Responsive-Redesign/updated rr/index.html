COVID-19 Travel Updates

As of May 23 2022, COVID-19 measures will only apply to travelers coming from a very high risk country. The U.S. is not considered a very high risk country. Fully vaccinated individuals can travel. A negative COVID-19 test result is no longer required. The details on the proof of vaccination are explained on the website of the U.S. Department of State.
