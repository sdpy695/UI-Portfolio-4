Build a Zoo

When you're shopping for animals to add to your zoo, do you ever get lost in the endless options? Do you ever wish there was a quick way to filter and sort by relevant categories like diet or number of legs?

I'm kidding. This is an exercise in using React.js to display data dynamically on a webpage, mimicking an online shopping interface.

The application allows users to look through a catalogue of zoo animals and compile a list of those they would like to purchase. It offers a sort feature and filter features, which can be layered ontop of eachother for users to quickly narrow their search through the catalogue.
